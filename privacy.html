Privacy Policy

Last updated May 16, 2026

The short version

SimpleTab collects only your email and the expense data you add. We don't sell, share, or look at your data. You own it. You can delete it anytime.

What we collect

SimpleTab collects only what's needed to run the app:

  • Email address — to create and manage your account when signing up with email, Apple, or Google.
  • Your expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and notes you add to the app. Stored securely and only accessible by you.

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to provide the app's features. We do not sell, share, or analyze your data for any commercial purpose. We do not show advertisements.

Where it's stored

Your data is stored on Google Firebase, encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Firebase security rules ensure only your authenticated account can read or write your data. Even we cannot view your individual expense data.

Sign-in methods

SimpleTab supports three sign-in methods: email/password, Sign in with Apple, and Sign in with Google. When you use Apple or Google sign-in, we receive only your email address (or a hidden Apple relay email if you choose). We never receive your Apple or Google passwords.

Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. Open SimpleTab → Settings → scroll down → tap Delete Account. This action is immediate and irreversible.

Children's privacy

SimpleTab is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the "Last updated" date above will change. Significant changes will also be noted in the app.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Visit simpletab.app and use the contact form. Messages come directly to me.
